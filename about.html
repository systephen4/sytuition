Meet the founder.

Introducing Stephen Mensah, the founder of ShapeYourTuition. Stephen is currently a first-year Physics undergraduate student at Imperial College London, which holds an impressive 6th place in the 2023 QS World University Rankings.

Stephen's academic prowess shines through with an outstanding achievement of 6 A-level (or equivalent) qualifications, earning A*A*A*AA and a Distinction.

Remarkably, Stephen achieved the highest scores in his cohort at St. Paul's Catholic School in Milton Keynes for Mathematics and Further Mathematics A-Levels. It's noteworthy that he completed Further Maths in less than one academic year, further amplifying this impressive achievement. Recognised as the top-achiever for A-levels in both 2021 and 2022 combined at his secondary school.

His burning desire for the pursuit of knowledge in education and academics, paired with his eagerness to assist others, drew him closer to the world of teaching, where he can now share his passion and enthusiasm while helping others along the way.
